First Field · Limited each season

Prove it on your own ground.

Each season we partner with a select group of growers to run First Field trials, with a dedicated advisor from start to harvest. Approved farms follow the Symm protocol to measure yield and nitrogen use, on their own acres, against their own baseline. Apply below.

How it works

01

Apply

Tell us about your operation. It takes a few minutes and helps us match you to the right trial.

02

Review

Our team reviews every application against the season's trial criteria and confirms your fit.

03

Onboard

Approved growers are paired with a dedicated advisor who walks you through everything, including any trial details, and receive the Symm protocol and trial plan.

04

Measure

Run a simple side-by-side trial and document yield and nitrogen results across the season.

What we look for

An active operation

You grow specialty or row crops and can commit acreage to a side-by-side trial this season or next.

A decision-maker

You own or help run the operation and can authorize a trial on your fields.

A measurement mindset

You are willing to follow the protocol and document yield and nitrogen data.

Representative ground

Fields that reflect your real growing conditions, so results translate to the rest of your farm.

What a First Field trial includes

Part 1 and Part 2

Your trial supply of both Symm inputs, resilience and nutrition, for the treated acres.

A dedicated advisor

Guides your trial from planning through harvest, with the protocol and a plan built around your fields.

A profitability report

After harvest, your results on your own acres, in dollars.

County exclusivity

A limited number of First Field trials are accepted per county each season.

What it costs

Standard 10-acre trials cost $500. That’s roughly $1,000 below full price. Each season we also fully sponsor a small number of trials, free to the farms we select.
